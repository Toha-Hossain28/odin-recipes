Mango shake

Refresh Your Summer with the blessing of Mangoes!

Mango Shake (Mango Milkshake) is a cool and tempting fruit drink prepared by simply blending ripe mango pieces, milk and sugar.

Ingredients

  • Ripe mangoes
  • Milk
  • Sugar or sweetener
  • Ice cubes (optional)
  • Vanilla ice cream or mango sorbet (optional)
  • Fresh mint leaves (optional)

Steps

  1. Peel the ripe mangoes and remove the pit. Cut the mango flesh into small cubes.
  2. Place the mango cubes into a blender or food processor.
  3. Add milk to the blender. The amount of milk can vary depending on how thick or thin you want your shake to be.
  4. Add sugar or a sweetener of your choice to the blender. Start with a small amount and adjust according to your desired sweetness.
  5. Optional: If you want a chilled shake, add a handful of ice cubes to the blender.
  6. Blend the mixture on high speed until everything is well combined and you have a smooth and creamy consistency.
  7. Taste the shake and adjust the sweetness or thickness by adding more sugar or milk if needed. Blend again to incorporate any additional ingredients.
  8. Optional: For added creaminess and flavor, you can add a scoop of vanilla ice cream or mango sorbet to the blender. This step is entirely up to your preference.
  9. Once blended to your desired consistency, pour the mango shake into glasses.
  10. Optional: Garnish each glass with a fresh mint leaf for added freshness and presentation.
  11. Serve the mango shake immediately and enjoy its refreshing taste!
